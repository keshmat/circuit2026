Circuit Points System

How circuit points are calculated for the Keshmat Chess Circuit 2026

Total Points Formula

TotalPoints = PlacementPoints + PerformanceBonus + ParticipationPoints

Placement
Based on final standing
+ Performance
Bonus for outperforming seed
+ Participation
Bonus for completing all rounds

Eligibility Requirement

To receive circuit points, a player must complete at least 50% of the tournament rounds.

Players who forfeit more than half of their games will receive 0 circuit points. This rule ensures that only active participants are rewarded.

Example: In a 7-round event, you must play at least 4 rounds to be eligible for points.

Performance Bonus

Earn bonus points for finishing higher than your seed (starting rank based on rating).

PerformanceBonus = min( 2 x max(0, SeedRank - FinalRank), cap )

In simple terms: You get +2 points for each position you climbed from your seed rank, up to a maximum cap.

Event Type Maximum Bonus Cap
Group A+20 points
Group B+20 points
Group C+25 points
Rapid+25 points
Example
If you're seeded 15th but finish 8th, you climbed 7 positions. Bonus = min(2 x 7, 25) = 14 points
Note: If you finish at or below your seed rank, the performance bonus is 0.

Participation Points

Bonus points awarded for completing all rounds of the tournament without forfeits.

Event Type Participation Bonus
Group A+5 points
Group B+5 points
Group C+3 points
Rapid+3 points
If you miss any round (forfeit), you receive 0 participation points.
