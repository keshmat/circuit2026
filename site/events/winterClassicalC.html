How Circuit Points are Calculated

Placement Points

Based on final standing percentile. Top 1% = 70pts, Top 3% = 63pts, etc.

Performance Bonus

2 points per position gained (seed - final rank). Open events: capped at 25 and 50% of placement. RR: capped at 20.

Participation

+5 points for completing all rounds.
